redact-ifier

a local CLI tool that takes messy medical documents — scanned PDFs, faxes, images — and turns them into clean, anonymized versions that are safe to share. everything runs on your machine, no cloud required.

uses OCR, NLP, and a two-model approach to detect and remove protected health information while keeping the medical content readable.
